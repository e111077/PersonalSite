Hello, my name is Elliott Marquez. I am a Junior at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology living in Simmons Hall. I was born and raised in San Antonio, Texas. There, I attended Northside Health Careers High School; a health-oriented magnet school ranked top in the city and top 150 nationally. Though I spent years learning medical skills and volunteering at area hospitals, I soon learned that my true passion was in mathematics and engineering. I spent over seven years in both middle school and high school taking part in state mathematics competitions and three years in state Latin competition specializing in Roman History.

I am an avid fan of Doctor Who with the 11th being my favorite. I am a huge fan of fast food (it is probably the Texan in me). I enjoy playing video games including but not limited to World of Warcraft, the Total War series, Pokemon, and the Nintendo classics (SMB, LoZ, etc.). I appreciate basketball since The Spurs are the only big sports team that can be found in San Antonio. I also love programming and learning new things whether it be computer-related, or just about world news.
