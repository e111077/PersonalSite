School

Hello, my name is Elliott Marquez. I am a junior at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology living in Simmons Hall. I was born and raised in San Antonio, Texas. There, I attended Northside Health Careers High School; a health-oriented magnet school ranked top in the city and top 150 nationally. Though I spent years learning medical skills and interning at area hospitals, I soon learned that my true passion was in mathematics and engineering. I spent over seven years in both middle school and high school taking part in state mathematics competitions and three years in state Latin competitions specializing in Roman History.

Interests

I love video games though I wish I had more time to play them. You can probably find me somewhere playing Pokémon, Super Smash Brothers, or something from the Total War Series. If I had the time, I would go back to feeding my high school World of Warcraft addiction. I love watching sports; I really love Basketball because coming from San Antonio, all we have is the San Antonio Spurs. When I watch soccer, I cheer for the Seattle Sounders, and for football I cheer for America's Team the Cowboys (even though it hurts to watch sometimes).
